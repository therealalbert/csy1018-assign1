This part is seperated to write something about NAMI Colllege. NAMI stands for Naaya Aayam Multi-Disciplinary Institute. NAMI is being produced as Nepal's premium Instructive Establishment in association with the College of Northampton (UN), United Kingdom. NAMI aims to convey world class education to Nepal. NAMI students will be enrolled as College of Northampton students. UN and NAMI staff will mutually convey all educational modules as planned by UN and affirmed by Government of Nepal. Environment at NAMI College is student friendly and the facility provided to students are amazing. Main academic goal of NAMI College is to provide quality education for the human development.
